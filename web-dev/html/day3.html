Gonda

Famous Landmarks

Laxman Giri Temple
A well-known religious place in Gonda.
It is visited by many devotees throughout the year.

Saryu Canal
An important canal near the city.
It plays a major role in irrigation and local life.

Devi Patan Temple
A famous Shakti Peeth located near Gonda.
It attracts pilgrims from nearby regions.
